Programme Overview

About the Scholarship

The DataWired and DataCamp scholarship is a strategic initiative of DataCamp Donates. It is a one-year, self-paced learning programme through DataCamp Premium, designed to promote learning in a chosen data specialism through free, high-quality education.

  • ✓ Full year of DataCamp Premium at no cost to you
  • ✓ Learn at your own pace around work and study commitments
  • ✓ Structured tracks, certifications, and portfolio projects
  • ✓ Peer accountability, mentorship, and community recognition

Career Tracks

Data Analyst

Collect, process, and analyse data to help businesses make informed decisions.

Data Scientist

Combine programming, statistics, and machine learning to extract actionable insights.

Machine Learning Scientist

Design and implement advanced algorithms and predictive models for AI applications.

Data Engineer

Build the infrastructure and pipelines that let data be collected, stored, and analysed.

Business Analyst

Bridge IT and business by using analytics to assess processes and deliver recommendations.

Analytics Engineer

Certifications
